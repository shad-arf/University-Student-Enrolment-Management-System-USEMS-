## 📝 2. Phase One: Initial Profile Form

### Form Submission
Upon the first login, students must complete their initial profile form. This form requires:
* Profile Image
* Full Name (in both Kurdish and English)
* Academic Range / Status
* Phone Numbers
* Faculty & Department

## 📂 3. Phase Two: Detailed University Documents Form

### Form Submission
After initial approval, students must submit their second form. This contains official university requirements, birthdate, and essential legal/academic documentation uploads:
* National Identification Card *(کارتی نیشتمانی)*
* Food Ration Card *(فۆرمی خۆراک)*
* Information Card *(کارتی زانیاری)*
* 12th Grade High School Certificate *(بڕوانامەی پۆلی ١٢)*
* Pledge / Commitment Letter *(بەڵێننامە)*
* Guarantor / Sponsorship Document *(کەفالەت)*
* Payment Receipt *(پسوولەی پارەدان)*

## 📊 4. Admin Dashboard & Management

The portal provides university staff and administrators with a centralized dashboard to monitor track analytics, manage registrations, and review multi-stage student applications.

### 📈 Overview Analytics
The primary admin dashboard tracks key registration metrics in real time, including:
* **Total Applications:** Overall volume of incoming profiles.
* **Pending States:** Dedicated queues for Phase One and Phase Two pending forms.
* **Approved / Registered Students:** Total active and finalized student accounts.
* **Rejected Applications:** Processed forms that did not meet institutional criteria.
* **Faculty Breakdown:** Visual distribution of records filtered across available academic faculties.
